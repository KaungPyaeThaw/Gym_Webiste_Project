Calisthenics Workouts

Muscle Up Pull-Ups

The muscle-up pull-up is an advanced calisthenics exercise that seamlessly combines a powerful pull-up with a dynamic transition to a dip. This challenging movement engages multiple muscle groups, including the back, biceps, shoulders, chest, and triceps, while requiring coordination and core stability. Mastering the muscle-up pull-up demonstrates impressive upper body strength, body control, and athleticism. Add this dynamic exercise to your routine for enhanced functional strength and overall fitness.

Handstand

The handstand is a foundational bodyweight exercise that involves supporting your body upside down with hands on the ground. It develops upper body strength, balance, and core stability. Incorporate handstands for improved shoulder strength, body awareness, and a dynamic element in your fitness routine.

Archer Push Ups

Archer push-ups are an advanced variation of the traditional push-up, targeting chest and shoulder muscles asymmetrically. By extending one arm laterally as you lower, they enhance upper body strength, especially in the chest and arms. Integrate archer push-ups for a challenging and effective workout that adds diversity to your routine.

Frog Stand

The frog stand is a foundational bodyweight exercise where you balance on your hands with knees resting on your elbows. This move develops wrist and shoulder strength while engaging the core. Incorporate frog stands for improved upper body stability and a unique addition to your fitness routine.

L Sit Hold

The L-sit hold is a challenging core exercise where you lift your legs to form an L shape while supporting your body on your hands. This move builds strong abdominal muscles and enhances overall core strength. Add L-sit holds for a powerful and effective core workout, elevating your strength and stability.
