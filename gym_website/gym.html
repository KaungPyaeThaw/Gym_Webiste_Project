Gym Workouts

Bench Press (Chest)

Welcome to the epitome of upper body strength training with the bench press – a time-tested exercise that takes center stage in sculpting a powerful chest, defined shoulders, and robust triceps. Our comprehensive guide is meticulously crafted to help you master the art of the bench press, unlocking the doorway to a stronger, more chiseled upper body.

You can also do incline bench press with barbell or dumbbell.

Squat (Legs)

Unleash the full potential of your lower body workout with the transformative benefits of weighted squats – a dynamic exercise that places the focus on your quads, hamstrings, glutes, and beyond. Our comprehensive guide is designed to help you master the art of weighted squats, paving the way for a stronger, more sculpted lower body.

You can also do hack squat or leg-press.

Rows (Back)

Discover the transformative power of the barbell bent-over row – a cornerstone exercise designed to sculpt and strengthen your upper back, including the latissimus dorsi, rhomboids, and traps. Our comprehensive guide is tailored to help you master the art of the barbell bent-over row, unlocking the key to a powerful and well-defined upper body.

Deadlift (Full Body)

Embark on a journey to build a powerful and sculpted back with the dynamic duo of pull-ups and inverted rows – two essential exercises that form the backbone of a comprehensive back workout. Our detailed guide is tailored to help you conquer these challenging movements, regardless of your fitness level, unlocking the key to a stronger, more defined back.

Overhead Press (Shoulder)

Embark on a journey to sculpt powerful shoulders and unleash upper body strength with the barbell overhead press. Our comprehensive guide is designed to help you master this classic strength training exercise, providing a pathway to building well-defined deltoids, enhancing triceps strength, and fostering overall upper body power.

You can also do with dumbbell.
