Home Workouts

Push Ups(Chest)

Elevate your fitness journey with the timeless and effective push-up, a fundamental bodyweight exercise that forms the cornerstone of strength training. Our push-up guide is designed to help you unlock the full potential of this versatile workout, targeting multiple muscle groups and enhancing overall upper body strength.

If you feel easy, you can do incline push-ups or pike push-ups.

Bodyweight Squat(Legs)

Embark on a journey to build powerful, toned legs with the simplicity of bodyweight squats – the go-to exercise for lower body strength and endurance. Our guide is crafted to help you master the art of this foundational movement, whether you're a fitness enthusiast or a beginner taking the first steps toward a stronger, healthier you.

If you feel easy, you can do pistol-squat.

Plank (core)

Embark on a transformative journey to fortify your core with the remarkable plank exercise – a fundamental movement that serves as the bedrock of a strong and stable midsection. Our comprehensive guide is crafted to guide you through the nuances of planking, helping you unlock the full potential of this versatile and effective core workout.

If you feel easy, you can do side plank.

Pull-Ups(Back)

Embark on a journey to build a powerful and sculpted back with the dynamic duo of pull-ups and inverted rows – two essential exercises that form the backbone of a comprehensive back workout. Our detailed guide is tailored to help you conquer these challenging movements, regardless of your fitness level, unlocking the key to a stronger, more defined back.

If you feel easy, you can do one-arm pull-ups.
